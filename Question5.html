Potato mash

  1. Buy 2 pounds of potato
  2. Peel 2 pounds of potato
  3. Bring Water to the boil
    1. Throw water in the pot
    2. put pot on cooker
    3. Switch cooker on
      1. Switch on the right plate
    4. Wait till it boils
  4. Boil them
  5. Mash them up when they are soft
