You are about to have in your hands a product that we have been thinking about for several months. We believe we have finally found the perfect balance between efficiency in use and design of our parallel bars. A noble wood added to qualitative processes and materials, all under our careful control. The product is offered in a delicate and worked box with the brand's design.

Besides the strategic choice of wood, we have left nothing to chance concerning the selection of materials! Dimensions worked to measure with our engineers, 4 centimeters in diameter for the bar and 23 centimeters in length in total! We opted for a very specific nesting system for our bars, lined with an extra strong glue to make it impossible to detach the bar from each of the two feet. Durable custom-made adhesive pads are present below each of the two feet of the parallel bars in order to obtain an optimal grip on the ground, whatever the coating! A special varnish is added to the wood, in a strategically chosen amount to protect the wood over time while leaving a coating that adheres slightly to the hand for perfect use.

It is in a quest for the best that our choice fell on beech wood. This wood is particularly renowned for its solidity, stability and aesthetics, properties that we were particularly looking for to produce our parallel bars. You will feel the Robustness of our bars made by hand from the first use and this, throughout the seasons!
